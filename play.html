    How to play

    • Win the round: empty your red-ringed Blitz pile.
    • The table: build the shared piles 1 → 10, one color each. Any 1 starts a new pile. First card down wins the spot.
    • Play a card: tap it — it jumps to the middle if it fits and scores.
    • Post piles: park spares on your 3 Posts — build down, alternating ▲ and ○. Empty Posts fill from your Blitz.
    • Stuck? FLIP turns 3 cards from your hand onto Wood. Run out? Flip again to recycle.
    • Score: +1 for each card you played to the middle, −2 for each card left in your Blitz.
